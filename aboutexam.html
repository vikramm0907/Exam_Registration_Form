Welcome to the official page dedicated to the upcoming examination!
This examination is designed to evaluate the knowledge, skills, and aptitude of candidates in various subjects.


Examination Details
Date: 28th December 2023
Time: 10 A.M to 1 P.M.
Venue: Hyderabad & Bangalore
Additional Note: Candidates should arrive 30 minutes prior to the exam for verification.

Examination Purpose
The examination aims to assess candidates' understanding and proficiency in their respective fields. It covers a wide range of topics and aims to provide a fair assessment of each participant's abilities.
Additional Note: The exam is based on the latest industry standards and academic curriculum.

Structure of the Examination
The examination will consist of multiple-choice questions, short answer questions, and essay-type questions. Each section is designed to test different aspects of the candidates' knowledge and analytical skills.
Additional Note: The exam duration is 3 hours with a short break between sections.

Preparation Tips
  • To excel in the examination, candidates are encouraged to:
    1. Review the syllabus thoroughly.
    2. Practice previous years' question papers.
    3. Manage time effectively during the examination.
    4. Stay calm and focused during the test.
    5. Join study groups for collaborative learning.
    6. Utilize online resources for additional practice.

Important Notes
  • Electronic devices such as mobile phones, tablets, and smartwatches are not allowed in the examination hall.
  • Candidates must bring:
    • Admit cards
    • A valid ID proof (e.g., driver's license, passport, or student ID)
    • Black or blue ballpoint pens for answering questions.
    • A water bottle (transparent and without labels).
